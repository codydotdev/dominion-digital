## [Role: Why It Matters]

[Narrative]
A template site has two problems once your business grows. First, it limits what you can do. When your process requires a custom workflow and the platform doesn't support it, you end up bending the business to fit the tool. Second, template sites carry technical debt. Every plugin, every workaround, every patch slows things down and creates failure points.

A custom build gives you a site that works the way your business works. Integrations that connect your tools without duct tape. Performance that doesn't degrade as you add pages. A foundation built for where you're going, not where you started.

[Punchline]
The right infrastructure compounds. The wrong one costs you every time something breaks.

## [Role: FAQ]

**How much does custom web development cost in Kansas City?**
Custom development ranges based on scope. A site with one or two integrations and custom pages runs differently than a full web application with a client portal and automated workflows. Dominion scopes every project before quoting so you know exactly what you're getting and what it costs before anything starts.

**How long does a custom build take?**
Most custom projects run 6 to 12 weeks from scoping to launch. More complex builds with multiple integrations take longer. Dominion sets a clear timeline during scoping and holds to it.

**Can you integrate my existing CRM, booking system, or other tools?**
Yes. Integration work is a standard part of most custom builds. If your tool has an API, it can be connected. Dominion handles the setup, testing, and documentation so your team doesn't have to figure it out after launch.
