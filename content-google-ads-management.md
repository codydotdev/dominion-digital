## [Role: Why It Matters]

[Narrative]
A Google Ads campaign managed for click volume produces traffic. A campaign managed for cost-per-lead produces business. Those are different goals that require different setups.

Quality Score is Google's rating of how relevant your keywords, ads, and landing pages are to each other. A high Quality Score lowers your cost per click. Lower cost per click means more leads for the same budget. A campaign built around buyer intent and matched to a converting page improves Quality Score over time. One built around volume does the opposite.

[Punchline]
Every point of Quality Score improvement lowers what you pay per click. The right setup compounds in your favor.

## [Role: FAQ]

**How much should I spend on Google Ads?**
It depends on your average customer value and your cost-per-lead target. Dominion.Digital works backward from what a new customer is worth to you, then sets a budget that can produce a positive return at a realistic cost-per-lead. Most Kansas City service businesses start in the $1,000–$3,000 per month range and scale from there.

**Do you manage ads on other platforms besides Google?**
Google Search is the primary platform for most Kansas City service businesses because it captures active buyers. For businesses that want broader reach, Dominion.Digital can extend into display and retargeting. Social ad management is handled through the PPC strategy service.

**How quickly will I start seeing leads?**
Google Ads can produce leads within the first week once campaigns go live, assuming conversion tracking and landing pages are set up correctly. Dominion.Digital prioritizes getting that foundation right before launch so the early data is clean and usable.
