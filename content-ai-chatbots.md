## [Role: Opening]

[Narrative]
A website that generates traffic but converts at 1% or 2% is leaving most of its potential on the table. Visitors land, read for a moment, and leave because they didn't find the specific answer they needed or because there was no easy way to take the next step. That gap is where leads disappear.

AI chatbots as part of an automation company's service stack are website-facing conversational agents that engage visitors in real time, answer their specific questions, qualify them as leads, and route them to an appointment or a form fill. They work the same at 11pm as they do at 11am.

Dominion.Digital builds AI chatbots configured to your services, your common questions, and your conversion goal — deployed on your site and connected to your calendar and CRM so every qualified conversation turns into a trackable lead.

A chatbot turns the question a visitor almost left with into the booking they stayed for.

## [Role: Why It Matters]

[Narrative]
Most service businesses treat their website as a brochure. A visitor lands, finds general information, and then has to decide whether to fill out a form or make a call. A significant portion of them don't do either. They leave, and that traffic is gone.

A chatbot changes the dynamic. The visitor is engaged before they decide to leave, guided to the specific information they were looking for, and given a direct path to booking. For businesses that rely on after-hours web traffic, a chatbot captures leads that would otherwise vanish into a "contact us" form that sits until morning.

[Punchline]
Your website has traffic. A chatbot determines how much of it converts.

## [Role: FAQ]

**Can the chatbot handle complex questions about my specific services?**
Yes, within the scope of what it's trained on. Dominion.Digital builds the chatbot's knowledge base around your services, your pricing structure, and your process. Questions outside that scope are handled with a graceful handoff: the bot collects the question and routes it to your team.

**Will it work on any website?**
Yes. The chatbot is deployed as a script that works on any website platform: WordPress, Webflow, Squarespace, custom builds, or Astro. It takes a single embed to get live.

**Does it replace my contact form?**
It complements it. Some visitors prefer forms. Others respond better to conversation. Having both means you capture leads either way. Dominion.Digital can configure the chatbot to offer the form as an option within the conversation flow.
