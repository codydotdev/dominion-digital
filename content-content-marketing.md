## [Role: FAQ]

**How long does it take for content to start ranking?**
New content typically starts picking up impressions within a few weeks of publishing and gains ranking momentum over the following months as Google indexes and evaluates it. Topical clusters tend to accelerate this: once Google recognizes your site as an authority on a subject, new pieces in that cluster rank faster.

**How much content do I need?**
It depends on your market and your goals. Dominion starts with a keyword research session to identify the highest-value opportunities and builds a content plan from there. Most Kansas City businesses start with one to two pieces per month and scale up as results compound.

**Do I need a separate content strategy for GEO?**
The same content serves both. Dominion structures every piece to rank in Google and to be cited by AI tools like ChatGPT and Perplexity. FAQ sections, direct-answer formatting, and schema markup are built into every article from the start.
