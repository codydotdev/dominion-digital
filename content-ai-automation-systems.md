## [Role: FAQ]

**What kinds of tasks can actually be automated?**
Lead follow-up, appointment booking, CRM updates, review request sequences, internal notifications, reporting, and content distribution are the most common starting points. If your team does a task on repeat that follows a consistent pattern, there's a good chance it can be automated. Dominion starts with a workflow audit to find the highest-value targets first.

**Do I need to replace my existing tools?**
No. Dominion builds around what you already use. Most automations connect via API or native integrations with tools like HubSpot, Salesforce, GoHighLevel, Google Workspace, and others. You keep your stack and we build the logic that connects it.

**How long does it take to set up?**
Most automation projects are live within 2 to 4 weeks. More complex builds with multiple workflows and custom integrations take longer. Dominion scopes every project before starting so you have a clear timeline before anything begins.
