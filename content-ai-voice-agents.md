Every AI voice agent engagement includes:

- **Lead qualification:** the agent asks your qualifying questions, collects contact information, and determines service fit before escalating or booking
- **Appointment booking:** qualified leads are booked directly into your calendar with confirmation sent automatically

- **CRM integration:** call outcomes, lead details, and transcripts are pushed to your CRM automatically so your team has full context before follow-up
- **Call transcripts and recordings:** every call is recorded and transcribed so you can review conversations and refine the agent over time

## [Role: FAQ]

**Will callers know they're talking to an AI?**
That depends on your preference. Dominion.Digital can build agents that are transparent about being AI or ones that are indistinguishable from a human receptionist. Most Kansas City businesses choose a natural-sounding agent without the AI disclosure, but both approaches are available.

**What happens if a caller asks something the agent can't handle?**
The agent is built with escalation logic. If a caller asks something outside the defined flow or specifically requests a human, the agent can take a message, offer a callback, or transfer the call depending on how the workflow is configured.

**What tools do you use to build voice agents?**
Dominion.Digital builds primarily on VAPI and Bland AI, depending on which platform fits the use case. Both support natural-sounding voices, real-time conversation, and the integrations your business needs.
