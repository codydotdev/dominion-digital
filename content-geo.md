## [Role: FAQ]

**What is GEO and how is it different from SEO?**
SEO is about ranking in traditional search results. GEO is about getting cited by AI tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews when they answer questions directly. The two overlap: content that ranks well in Google tends to be cited more by AI tools. GEO adds a layer of structure and formatting on top of SEO to make your content easy for AI systems to extract and quote.

**Which AI platforms does GEO cover?**
Dominion tracks and optimizes for the platforms your customers are most likely using: Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, and Perplexity. Coverage expands as new platforms gain adoption. The content and structure work done for one platform generally carries over to others.

**Does my business need both SEO and GEO?**
For most Kansas City businesses, yes. SEO covers traditional Google rankings. GEO covers AI search. Dominion runs them together because the content strategy is shared. Doing both at once is more efficient than treating them as separate projects.
