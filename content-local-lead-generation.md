## [Role: FAQ]

**How quickly will I start seeing leads?**
Google Ads typically produces leads within the first week once campaigns go live. SEO takes longer to build momentum but compounds over time. Most Kansas City businesses see a meaningful increase in total lead volume within the first 60 days when both channels are running together.

**How is this different from just running Google Ads on my own?**
The difference is in the full system. Ads alone send traffic to pages that may not convert. SEO alone misses buyers who are ready to call right now. Dominion builds both channels and connects them to tracked landing pages, so every dollar spent is accountable and every lead is attributed to a source.

**What kinds of businesses does this work for?**
Local service businesses in Kansas City with a defined service area and a consistent average job value. Contractors, home services, professional services, medical and wellness, and similar businesses where a new customer has clear, measurable value. Dominion does a lead audit before starting to confirm the numbers make sense.
