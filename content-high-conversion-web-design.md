## [Role: Why It Matters]

[Narrative]
If your site gets 500 visitors a month and converts at 1%, you get 5 leads. Fix the conversion rate to 3% and you get 15 leads, same traffic, no increase in ad spend. That gap is real money, and most Kansas City business owners haven't addressed it yet.

The reason most sites underperform has nothing to do with the amount of traffic. It is the design. Visitors land, feel confused or unimpressed, and leave. A high-converting site is structured to catch that drop-off, answer the visitor's question before they ask it, and make the next step obvious.

That is the lever. And it works on the traffic you already have.

[Punchline]
More leads from the same traffic. That is what conversion-focused design actually delivers.

## [Role: FAQ]

**What makes a website "high-converting"?**
A high-converting website removes friction between the visitor and the action you want them to take. Clear calls to action, a layout that guides attention where it needs to go, fast load times, and copy that speaks directly to what the visitor cares about. Most sites fail on at least two of those. Fixing them is what moves the needle.

**How long does a high-conversion web design project take?**
Most projects run 3 to 6 weeks from discovery to launch. The timeline depends on the number of pages and how quickly feedback rounds move. Dominion runs a structured process so nothing stalls.

**Do you write the copy, or do I need to provide it?**
Dominion handles the copy. Every page is written with conversion in mind. If you have existing messaging you want to keep, we'll review it and flag anything that's working against you before it goes live.

**Does the site come with SEO built in?**
Yes. Every Dominion site is built with clean code, proper heading structure, and on-page SEO signals from day one. If you want to go further with ongoing SEO or local search, those services are available through [Dominion's SEO service](/internet-marketing/seo).
