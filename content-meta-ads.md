[Scope / Inclusion List]
Every Meta Ads engagement includes:

- **Audience build and targeting:** custom and lookalike audiences built from your customer data, interest targeting configured for your service category, and geographic targeting set to your actual service area
- **Campaign structure and setup:** ad account audit (or fresh setup), campaign and ad set structure built around your offer and conversion goal, with budget allocation designed for efficient CPL from day one
- **Ad creative strategy:** copy and creative direction for each ad set, including primary text, headline, and image or video direction tailored to the platform and audience
- **Lead generation form or landing page routing:** either native Meta lead forms (highest conversion rate on mobile) or traffic routed to a dedicated landing page, whichever produces a better cost per lead for your offer
- **Retargeting sequences:** separate campaigns targeting people who have engaged with your ads or visited your site but haven't converted, keeping your business visible through the decision window
- **Monthly reporting:** cost per lead, reach, frequency, and click-through rate reviewed monthly with plain-language interpretation and optimization notes

## [Role: FAQ]

**How is Meta Ads different from Google Ads?**
Google Ads targets people who are already searching for what you offer. Meta Ads target people who match the profile of your customer based on demographics, interests, behaviors, and location. Google captures existing demand. Meta creates demand from a cold audience. Both channels have a role, and the strongest lead pipelines use both.

**What budget do I need to run Meta Ads?**
For most Kansas City service businesses, a starting test budget of $500 to $1,500 per month produces enough data to evaluate performance and optimize. Campaigns with smaller budgets take longer to gather signal and may not reach statistical significance. Dominion.Digital will tell you upfront what budget makes sense for your goal and market before anything is built.

**Do you handle the ad creative, or do I need to provide it?**
Dominion.Digital handles copy and creative direction. For static image ads, creative is produced as part of the engagement. For video ads, Dominion.Digital provides direction and scripting. If you already have brand video assets, those get reviewed and formatted for the platform.
